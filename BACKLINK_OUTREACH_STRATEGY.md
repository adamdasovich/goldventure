## Alternative Link Building Strategies

### **1. HARO (Help A Reporter Out)**
- Sign up: https://helpareporter.com
- Respond to mining/finance/investment queries
- Provide expert commentary with link attribution

### **2. Industry Directory Submissions**
**Free Directories**:
- Crunchbase: https://crunchbase.com
- Product Hunt: https://producthunt.com
- AngelList: https://angel.co
- StartupBlink: https://startupblink.com

**Paid Mining Directories**:
- Mining Business Directory
- InvestorIntel Directory

### **3. Content Partnerships**
- Offer to provide data for mining industry reports
- Collaborate on educational webinars
- Provide expert quotes for industry articles

### **4. Resource Page Link Building**
Search Google for:
- "junior mining resources"
- "mining investment tools"
- "NI 43-101 resources"
- "TSXV stock analysis tools"

Contact sites with resource pages and request inclusion.

### **5. Broken Link Building**
- Find broken links on mining websites (use Ahrefs or Check My Links extension)
- Offer your relevant content as replacement
- Focus on educational/glossary pages

## Important Guidelines

### **Do's**:
✅ Personalize every email
✅ Provide genuine value first
✅ Follow up once (not more)
✅ Be professional and concise
✅ Offer exclusive access or collaboration

### **Don'ts**:
❌ Mass email without personalization
❌ Be overly promotional
❌ Demand or expect links
❌ Follow up more than twice
❌ Use automated outreach tools initially
